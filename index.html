Publishing principles

  • Independence: no paid influence. Readers come first.
  • Verification: clear distinction between fact, analysis, and opinion.
  • Corrections: visible, time-stamped, and accountable.
  • Safety and dignity: no doxxing. No hate. No sensationalized “trauma marketing”.
  • Transparency: clear funding disclosures and separation of editorial decisions.
