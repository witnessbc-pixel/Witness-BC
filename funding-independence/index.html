Funding & independence

Support the work without owning it.

WitnessBC is reader-supported. Financial support keeps the lights on, but it does not buy coverage, outcomes, or favourable treatment.

No charitable tax receipt. Tips are not tax-deductible. This page describes editorial boundaries, not legal advice.

What support does

Support funds infrastructure and the ability to work independently.

  • Reporting capacity: time for verification, documents, and outreach.
  • Publishing capacity: hosting, tools, and production.
  • Safety capacity: privacy discipline, secure workflows, and risk review.

What support does not do

Support never changes editorial decisions.

  • No guaranteed story: tips do not trigger coverage.
  • No approval rights: supporters do not review drafts.
  • No outcomes: no promises on investigations, timelines, or targets.

If a supporter has a direct stake in a topic, coverage may be declined or handled with stricter conflict controls.

Independence rules

1) No pay-for-coverage

Money cannot purchase stories, silence, framing, or “balanced” treatment.

2) Conflict discipline

Material conflicts are disclosed, managed, or the work is declined.

3) Corrections are visible

Corrections are dated and specific. See /corrections/.

Receipts & refunds

Tips are treated as support for publishing operations (not charity donations). For payment questions, email support@witnessbc.com.
