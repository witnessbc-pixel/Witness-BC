Quick checklist

  • Remove unique time + place combinations.
  • Remove exact job titles if they identify a single person.
  • Replace rare details with category-level descriptions.
  • Do not publish private IDs, signatures, or full addresses.
  • If unsure: keep the record private; publish the pattern.
