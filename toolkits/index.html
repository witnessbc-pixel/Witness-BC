  • Creators and writers who publish claims and want cleaner sourcing.

What problems this prevents

  • Claims written without evidence mapping.
  • Sources lost over time or links dying without a private record.
  • Accidental identification from detail-combination leaks (time + place + role).
  • Silent edits and messy updates that destroy credibility later.
Goal: publish what is defensible, label limits clearly, reduce avoidable risk, and correct cleanly.
