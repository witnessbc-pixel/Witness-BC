What this prevents (in plain language)

  • Evidence drift: claims outlive the sources that supported them.
  • Link rot: important URLs die without a private index.
  • Identity leakage: “time + place + role” combinations identify people unintentionally.
  • Silent edits: changes that later destroy credibility because they were not logged.
Goal: publish what is defensible, label limits clearly, reduce avoidable risk, and correct cleanly.
